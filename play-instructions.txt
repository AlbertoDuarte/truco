Possible moves:
  A - play card on the left.
  S - play card on the middle.
  D - play card on the right.
  T - ask truco (round start with 1 point and goes to 3, 6, 9 and 12).
You can only ask truco on the round if the last team who called truco was the opposite team (or no one asked)
T also accepts truco if opponent asked it.
  Q - gives up round if opponent asked truco.

You need to press a key for the AI to play (any key).

To play, run 'python3 play.py' and specify AI version file when asked.
